Spooky!

The book said that in order to talk to that ghost you have to be in the right place at the right time.

But where is that place, you might ask. At the end of the page there are coordinates which lead to the castle's backyard. You immediately close the book

and run outside. All of a sudden the ground starts shaking and you see a dark figure appear out of nowhere. But it is not a ghost, it is something much

different and far more powerful. It is a shadow that can take different shapes and forms and has no physical presence. You slowly put your hand towards it trying

to make contact but as soon as you reach out you start having visions. That is the creature talking to you. In your visions you see a big cave full of

dragons and they all seem to be gathering around some kind of a stone as if like they are protecting it. From the outside, the cave is surrounded by an infinite

number of sand watches and it seems that they are all showing the same time. Then your vision ends and the dark creature vanishes.
