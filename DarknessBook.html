Dark Matters.

You decide to pursue the darkest weapon in the priest world, The Shadow Dagger. You find a book in the

castle's library which says that this weapon posseses powers that have never been seen in this world. But in order to make the first step to finding

it, you must talk to a ghost that once was the last owner of the Shadow Dagger. You have to carefully listen to his instructions, because if the weapon

falls in the wrong hands, there will be consequences around the whole world.
