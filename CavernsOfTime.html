Fire breathers!

Months have passed, but you can't stop thinking about your meeting with the "ghost". You decide to start researching

and find the location of that cave and explore what's inside. Eventually you come across an ancient rune which when placed under moonlight, shows the location

of the thing you desire most. You immediately pack your stuff and set off. You find the cave in the middle of a desert and it looks exactly as described in the book.

You send a few prayers before going in, not knowing what you might stumble across. As soon as you walk in, a giant rock falls behind you and blocks your way out.

You have no other option but to go down the giant staircase that has appeared in fron of you. The deeper you go, the colder it gets. After a couple of hours

you still haven't reached the bottom, you get dizzy and pass out. The next thing you see after you wake up is the giant fire breathers sitting around the stone,

but something isn't right. They don't even acknowledge your presence and it seems like something has possessed them. You slowly approach the stone which

is placed on a big pile of skulls. It looks horrific. All of a sudden, you start hearing voices which get louder and louder as if like they are trying to

drive you crazy. Trying to cover your ears, you stumble and push the stone out of its position. It falls right on your chest.
