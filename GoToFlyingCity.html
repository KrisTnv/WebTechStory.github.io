The Flying City of Dalaran.

You summon a portal and travel to Dalaran - The Flying City. When you arrive there, you see the streets overwhelmed with

dead bodies. You come across an old man sitting on the porch in front of his burning house. You ask him what happened and he says that a powerful shadow

weighs upon Dalaran and is killing every living thing in it's path. He says that if no one comes with reinforcements, the city will be taken over. All of a sudden,

seing that you hold the Light's Wrath, Agrammar comes out of the shadows and says that even with the most sacred weapon no one will be able to stop him.

He reveals his cruel plan on how to take over the Flying City and then the whole world.
